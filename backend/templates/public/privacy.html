Privacy Notice

For the full technical explanation of Gmail data handling, see Security & Privacy.

What we collect

Account details you provide (username, email, hashed password), email metadata and content needed to classify and investigate messages, uploaded .eml reports, and audit log entries recording administrator and system actions.

Gmail data

With a connected mailbox, Sentinel retrieves message content via the Gmail API to classify it, and applies labels for Legitimate/Needs Review/Phishing states. An encrypted OAuth refresh token is stored to maintain the connection — never your Google password.

Retention

Message bodies are purged after a fixed retention window, retaining only classification metadata. Reports and audit log entries are retained for the operation of the platform.

Who can see what

Security Administrators can see organisation-wide detections and connected-mailbox status. Employees can see only their own submitted reports.

Third parties

Sentinel uses Google's Gmail API and OAuth for mailbox access, and may use an email-sending provider for verification/reset emails and administrator notifications, if configured. No data is sold or shared for advertising.

Your choices

You can request your account be reviewed by an administrator, and a connected mailbox's owner can disconnect it at any time, revoking stored access immediately.

Contact

Questions about this notice can be sent via the Project Enquiry page.
