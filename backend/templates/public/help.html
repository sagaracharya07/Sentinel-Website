Help Centre

Get the most out of Sentinel

Practical guidance for connecting Gmail, understanding verdicts, and reporting suspicious mail.

Connecting Gmail

Sign in as a Security Administrator, open Connected Mailboxes, and click Connect Gmail. Grant the requested permissions, and protection activates automatically once Sentinel verifies its labels.

Gmail permissions explained

Sentinel requests only the scopes needed to read messages and manage labels — see Security & Privacy for the full breakdown.

Understanding verdicts

Legitimate, Needs Review, and Phishing — plus what phishing probability and prediction confidence actually mean. See the Threat Lab and the portal's Security Guide.

Reviewing incidents

Open any detection to see sender identity, authentication results, links, attachments, and the model's explanation — then confirm, release, or escalate.

Reporting suspicious email

From the user portal, upload the original .eml file (recommended) or use Quick Analysis for a faster, more limited pasted-text check.

Connection troubleshooting

Use Test Connection on the Connected Mailboxes page to verify credentials and labels. If it fails, Reconnect to refresh Google's consent.
