Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026 — describes this build's actual data handling, not generic boilerplate.

What we collect

Account information you provide directly (username, email address, password — stored as a one-way hash, never in plaintext). If you connect a mailbox for monitoring, we process the content of messages in that mailbox — sender, subject, and body — solely to classify them.

How long we keep it

Scan metadata (classification, confidence score, risk level, timestamp) is retained for auditing and to support the feedback/retraining loop. The raw body content of a scanned email is automatically redacted 24 hours after the scan by an automated process — after that point, only the classification outcome remains, not the original content.

Who can see it

Scan results are visible to the user who submitted them and to administrators reviewing the quarantine/flag queue. Mailbox credentials are stored only as configuration for the connection itself and are never displayed back through the interface.

Third parties

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Outbound email (account verification, password reset) is sent through a configured SMTP provider solely to deliver that specific message.

Your choices

You can request account deletion at any time by contacting us. If you connected a mailbox, disconnecting it (removing the configuration) stops all further access immediately — nothing about the integration persists once credentials are removed.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the contact form.
