Platform capabilities

Built around the functional requirements that matter for a real deployment.

Explainable scoring

Every verdict comes with the specific phrases, links and sender signals that drove the score — not just a label.

Automatic quarantine

High-risk email is held before delivery, with borderline messages flagged for human review instead of blocked outright.

Feedback & retraining

False positives and false negatives are corrected in one click and logged to improve detection over time.

Full audit trail

Every scan, decision and admin action is timestamped and logged for compliance and review.

Confidence scoring

A 0–100 score and risk band (Low / Medium / High) accompany every classification.

Lightweight deployment

No proprietary lock-in or enterprise licensing — designed to fit small and mid-sized teams.
